Welcome to the "Machine Generated Recipes"

The role of intelligence in creativity of machines has been a subject of discussion since long. Many systems are developed to create recipes using machine learning algorithms, including Neural networks, Evolutionary algorithms and many other. We encourage everyone to share recipes generated by machine learning methods to help other researchers to compare their recipes with already created recipes. Some systems to automate the process of recipe creation are following:
EvoChef
EMMA
IBM Chef Watson
